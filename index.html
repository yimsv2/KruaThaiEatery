About Us

Since 2006, Krua Thai Eatery has been a beloved Ravenna, Seattle restaurant, serving up an extensive menu of authentic Thai foods. Whether you're craving the rich, aromatic flavors of our Green Curry or the perfectly balanced tastes of our classic Pad Thai, we offer a diverse selection, including delicious gluten-free options, all available for both dine-in and pick-up.

we look forwards to welcoming you everday from day till night.
